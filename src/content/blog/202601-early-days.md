title: "Early Days: From IT Support to Web Development"
date: 2026-01-10
---
My journey into technology really started to take shape during my time at Telkom University, but it was my early professional experiences that truly cemented my path. 

In early 2024, I took on an IT Support role at Syaamil Group. It wasn't just about fixing computers; I was tasked with indexing 600 pages of the Quran using a web base for their Qursa Application. This project required a lot of attention to detail and gave me a profound appreciation for how technology can preserve and organize important information. 

Later on, around late 2025, I joined the Government Research and Development team in Bandung as a Web Developer. Here, I had the opportunity to design and develop the SatuDesk website from the ground up, tailored specifically to user needs. It was an incredible learning experience that pushed me to think about user experience and solid web architecture. These early steps laid the foundation for everything I would build next.
