title: "Building Applications: My Journey as a Full-Stack Developer"
date: 2026-02-15
---
Building software that solves real problems is what I enjoy the most. During my time as a Software Engineering Intern at PT Informatika Media Pratama, I got my hands dirty with real-world applications. 

One of my favorite projects was **CariProject**, a freelance marketplace. I built the entire stack using Django and PostgreSQL, deployed it with Docker, and integrated Google Auth and the Midtrans payment gateway. It was a massive learning curve, but seeing the whole platform come together—from user monitoring to the admin dashboard—was incredibly rewarding.

I also spent a lot of time working on **Prenava**, a maternal and child health app built with Laravel, React, and Flutter. It had complex features like a pregnancy calculator, community threads, and even an integrated shop. And then there was **Hawa**, a real-time weather dashboard I built using FastAPI and Vite React. Adding WhatsApp integration for early weather warnings was a fun challenge that taught me a lot about real-time notifications and external APIs.

I love the process of taking an idea and turning it into a functioning, reliable system. Every project has taught me something new, whether it's about backend scalability, frontend responsiveness, or integrating complex third-party services.
