title: "Navigating the World of IT Governance and Compliance"
date: 2026-05-20
---
While I love writing code, I've also discovered a strong passion for IT Governance, Risk, and Compliance (IT GRC). Code is only as good as the policies and security standards that govern it.

My first deep dive into this was a project designing a COBIT 2019 framework for PT Pegadaian Bandung. I focused on prioritizing governance objectives, analyzing capability gaps, and creating audit-ready deliverables. This experience opened my eyes to the importance of standardized requirements and change acceptance criteria.

This passion led me to an IT GRC role at PT Perkom Indah Murni in Jakarta. Here, I managed IT policies and ensured our managed services complied with strict standards like ISO 27001 and ITIL. I was conducting risk identification, supporting audit processes, and finding ways to automate compliance reporting. 

It's fascinating to sit at the intersection of technology and business strategy. Ensuring that a company's infrastructure is not only functional but also secure and compliant is a challenge I thoroughly enjoy tackling.
